This is a tool about queering how we teach computer science in K-12 spaces. Some questions that arose from this project were: how come STEM is predominantly geared towards and privileges cis men? How do we ensure that voices of queer and trans youth of color enter these spaces, especially at a young age? What does it look like to create a space for marginalized folks, and evoke belonging from the very beginning?
